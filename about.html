Overview

Digital Radio Plus has been operating for 20 years, broadcasting channels using the highly popular DAB+ transmission technology. The company started as a small radio studio, operating in regional Australia. Over the years the radio studio has passed through a number of transformations.

Our Mission

As the reputation of the studio grew nationally, and production expanded to meet the popular demand, the structure of the business changed to its present form. The production staff increased and currently includes 3 production managers (programme controllers), 30 full-time staff including composers, editors, journalists, presenters and sound engineers.
